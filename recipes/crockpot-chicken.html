Crockpot Chicken Breast Recipe

Descripton

This delicious crockpot chicen breast recipe is a great base for a ton of different lunches, dinners or for weekly meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 4 large chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Pour the Chicken broth in the slow cooker. (1/2 cup of chicken broth)
  2. Combine the seasonings in a small dish. (1 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon onion powder, 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme, 1/2 teaspoon paprika, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder.)
  3. Rub the olive oil and seasoning over the chicken breasts. (2 tablesppons olive oil, 4 large chicken breasts.)
  4. Place the chicken breasts in the slow cooker.
  5. Cook on high for 3-4 hours or on low for 4-6 hours.
