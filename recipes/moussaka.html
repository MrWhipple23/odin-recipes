Moussaka Recipe

Description

This moussaka tastes great!! This has layers of eggplant that is baked in a ground beef sauce and Lathered in a creamy white suace in the classic greek dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplants, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespons dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, divided
  • 1(8ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ground white peper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  • Step 1

    Lay eggplant slices on paper towels, sprinkle lightly with salt. Let sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture, then pat dry with paper towels.

  • Step 2

    Warm olive oil in a skillet over high meat. Fry eggplant until browned, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels; set aside.

  • Step 3

    Melt 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in groud beef, onions, and garlic; season with salt and black pepper. Cook and stir until beef is browned, 8 to 10 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Add parsley, fine herbs, cinnamon, and 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg. Pour in tomato sauce and wine; mix well. Simmer for 20 minutes. Allow to cool, then stir in beaten egg.

  • Step 5

    Scald milk in a saucepan over medium heat.

  • Step 6

    At the same time, melt 1/2 cup buteer in a large skillet over medium heat.

  • Step 7

    Whisk flour into butter until smooth. Lower heat; gradually pour in scaled milk, whisking constantly until it thickens. Season white sauce with salt and white pepper.

  • Step 8

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  • Step 9

    Arrage a single layer of eggplant in the prepared baking dish.

  • Step 10

    Cover eggplant with meat sauce, then sprinkle 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese on top. Cover with remaining eggplant and sprinkle another 1/2 cup cheese on top.

  • Step 11

    Pour White sauce on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg. Cover with remaining cheese.

  • Step 12

    Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and brown, about 1 hour.

  • Step 13

    Serve hot and enjoy!
