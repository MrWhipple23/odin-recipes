Moussaka Recipe

Description

This moussaka tastes great!! This has layers of eggplant that is baked in a ground beef sauce and Lathered in a creamy white suace in the classic greek dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggplants, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespons dried parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg, divided
  • 1(8ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ground white peper, to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
